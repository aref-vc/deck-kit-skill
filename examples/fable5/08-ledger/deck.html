About the model

Fable 5 is the first model in Anthropic's Claude 5 family and the first of a new Mythos-class tier that sits above Opus. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 are the same underlying model: Fable 5 ships with safeguards for general use, Mythos 5 has those safeguards lifted for trusted partners.

The names mark the safety difference, not a capability one. Fable from the Latin fabula, Mythos from the Greek mythos.

Model id claude-fable-5 · Knowledge cutoff Jan 2026

Mythos-class sits a tier above Opus. Safety note: under 5% of sessions fall back to Opus 4.8.

A tier above Opus. State of the art on nearly every tested benchmark, with the largest lead on longer, more complex tasks. The biggest gains are on long-horizon work that holds context across many steps.
