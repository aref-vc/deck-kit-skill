Claude Fable 5 · Release Report03 / 12

About

Fable 5 is the first model in Anthropic's Claude 5 family and the first of a new Mythos-class tier that sits above Opus. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 are the same underlying model: Fable 5 ships with safeguards for general use, while Mythos 5 has those safeguards lifted for trusted partners. The names come from Latin "fabula" and Greek "mythos" and mark the safety difference, not a capability difference.

Model id claude-fable-5 · Knowledge cutoff January 2026

Under 5% of sessions trigger a fallback to Opus 4.8. Three classifier domains guard cybersecurity, biology and chemistry, and distillation. Over 1,000 hours of external bug-bounty testing found no universal jailbreaks.
