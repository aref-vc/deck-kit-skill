About

Fable 5 is the first model in the Claude 5 family and the first of a new Mythos-class tier that sits above Opus. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 are the same model: Fable 5 ships with safeguards for general use, while Mythos 5 lifts them for trusted partners. The names mark the safety difference, not a capability difference.

Knowledge cutoff Jan 2026 · Model id claude-fable-5

Under 5% of sessions trigger a fallback to Opus 4.8. Three classifier domains: cybersecurity, biology and chemistry, distillation. Over 1,000 hours of external bug-bounty testing found no universal jailbreaks.
