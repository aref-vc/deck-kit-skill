01 · About

A new tier

Fable 5 is the first model in the Claude 5 family and the first of a new Mythos-class tier that sits above Opus. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 are the same underlying model. Fable ships with safeguards for general use; Mythos has those safeguards lifted for trusted partners. The pairing marks the safety difference, not a capability difference.
