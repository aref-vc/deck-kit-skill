## 4. About
- Fable 5 is the first model in Anthropic's Claude 5 family and the first of a new "Mythos-class" tier
  that sits above Opus.
- Fable 5 and Mythos 5 are the same underlying model. Fable 5 ships with safeguards for general use;
  Mythos 5 has those safeguards lifted for trusted partners.
- The names: Fable from Latin "fabula", Mythos from Greek "mythos" — the pairing marks the safety
  difference, not a capability difference.
- Context: knowledge cutoff January 2026. Model id claude-fable-5.
