Field Studio · 202604 / 12

The field

What we paint

Open country, water, and weather. We choose places where the light does most of the work and the scene keeps moving.

Why outdoors

A photograph flattens light. Standing in it does not. Painting on site keeps the colour true to the moment.

How we paint

Look first

Sit with the scene before the brush.

Block in

Lay the big shapes and values early.

Chase light

Work fast while the light holds.

Mix true

Match the colour you actually see.

Stop early

Leave it before it goes muddy.
