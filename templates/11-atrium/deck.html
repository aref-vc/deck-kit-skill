How we work

Every engagement runs the same five steps. Each one ends in a decision the client owns, not a deck on a shelf.

01 Frame

Agree the question and the call the work must answer.

02 Diagnose

Read the market, the numbers, and the field. Find the cause.

03 Design

Shape two or three real options, costed and ranked.

04 Decide

Put the choice to the board with the trade-offs named.

05 Deliver

Stand up the change and stay until it holds.
